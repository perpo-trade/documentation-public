title: "Accounts"
description: "Account structure"
---

On Perpo, each wallet gets a **separate account per front-end (DEX)**. Your account address is derived from your wallet address and the builder ID of the front-end you're trading on.

## What this means

If you trade on DEX A and DEX B with the same wallet, you will have two independent accounts. Each account is fully isolated:

- **Trades** placed through DEX A cannot be seen or modified through DEX B.
- **Fees, volume, and fee tiers** are tracked separately per account.
- **No shared state** exists between accounts, even if the same wallet owns both.
