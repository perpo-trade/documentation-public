# How to become a Builder on Orderly Network

Welcome! If you are looking to launch a trading application, earn fees, and leverage the infrastructure of an omnichain orderbook, you are in the right place.

Becoming a Builder on Orderly Network allows you to deploy high-performance trading solutions while tapping into our deep shared liquidity. This guide walks you through the full onboarding process, from initial contact to choosing your deployment method.

---

## Step 1: Get in touch with the Orderly team

Start by reaching out to us through our official channels. You can connect with a business development manager via [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/orderlynetwork) or [Telegram](https://t.me/Orderly_Discussions), or submit an inquiry through the [Orderly Network website](https://orderly.network).

Once connected, our team will help you navigate the technical and commercial requirements of your Builder profile.

---

## Step 2: Understanding the fee model

Orderly operates on a "base \+ margin" model, granting you full pricing autonomy:

- **Orderly base fee:** This is the foundational cost retained by Orderly. It starts at **3 bps (0.03%) for taker** trades and **0 bps for maker** trades.
- **Builder margin:** You define the final fee charged to your end-users. Any amount set above the Orderly base fee is **100% your profit**.

**Example:**\
If your base taker fee is 3 bps and you set your user-facing fee to 6 bps, you earn **3 bps** in profit on every taker trade.

[Learn more about trading fees and revenue share](/introduction/trade-on-orderly/trading-basics/trading-fees)

---

## Step 3: Choose your build method

There are two primary ways to launch your platform. Your choice impacts your technical overhead and initial setup costs.

### Option A: Orderly One (low-code)

The fastest path to market. Orderly One is a "DEX-in-a-box" solution that lets you launch a fully branded trading interface in minutes.

- **Graduation fee:** Launching is free, but a **\$1,000 fee** is required to enable revenue sharing and receive your unique builder code. You can pay in USDC, or receive a **25% discount** by paying in \$ORDER.
- **Best for:** Rapid deployment and teams wanting a professional perp DEX without custom frontend development.

[Explore Orderly One](https://dex.orderly.network/dex)

### Option B: Custom SDK/API integration

Build a bespoke trading experience tailored to your specific brand and user needs.

- **Graduation fee:** Not applicable (standard integration).
- **Best for:** Wallets, existing DEXs adding perpetuals, or Builders creating unique, highly-tailored trading journeys.
- **Setup:** Connect your custom frontend to our orderbook using Orderly's robust SDKs and APIs.

---

## Step 4: Provide initial Builder information

<Note>
  **Note:** This step applies specifically to Builders opting for **Custom SDK/API integration**. For Orderly One users, these details are collected automatically during the graduation process.
</Note>

To initialize your profile, you must provide the following:

- **Builder ID:** (e.g., `my_dex_identifier`)
- **Builder name:** (e.g., `MyDEX`)
- **Default taker fee:** 0.06% (6 bps)
- **Default maker fee:** 0.03% (3 bps)

After receiving these details (and processing the Orderly One graduation fee, if applicable), the Orderly team will:

- Register your Builder profile.
- Assign your unique builder ID.
- Configure your initial fee levels and permissions.

This typically takes **1-2 business days**. You will be notified once your profile is live.

---

## Step 5: Public tier vs. staking tier

Determine your starting tier in the **Builder Staking Program** to maximize your margins:

- **Public tier:** No volume or staking requirements (Base taker fee: 3 bps).
- **Staking/volume tiers:** Stake \$ORDER tokens or meet volume thresholds to instantly lower your base fee, increasing your take-home profit.

[View the Builder Staking Program tiers and benefits](/introduction/trade-on-orderly/trading-basics/trading-fees#builder-staking-programme)

---

## Step 6: Register your admin account

Once your profile is ready, you must register your **admin account** to manage settings and collect your earned trading fees.

### Registration for Orderly One Builders

1. Complete the graduation flow on the [Orderly One portal](https://dex.orderly.network/dex).
2. **Secure your keys:** You will receive an Orderly public and private key.
3. **Final linkage:** Securely share your **wallet public address** (not the private key) with the Orderly team.

### Registration for custom SDK/API Builders

1. Visit the [builder registration tool](https://github-dev.orderly.network/broker-registration/).
2. Connect your wallet and complete the registration flow.
3. **Secure your keys:** You will receive an Orderly public and private key.
4. **Final linkage:** Securely share your **wallet public address** (not the private key) with the Orderly team.

Once the linkage is complete, you can access the [Perpo admin portal](https://admin.orderly.network).

# FAQs

### Can I change my user fees later?

Yes. You can modify your maker and taker fees at any time through the [admin portal](https://admin.orderly.network).

### Can I switch from the public tier to a staking tier later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade your tier at any time by staking \$ORDER or reaching the required aggregate volume thresholds in the **Builder Staking Program**.
