## 2026 H1 focus

The 2026 H1 roadmap targets four tracks — growth, trading capabilities, platform and developer tooling, and vault upgrades — each designed to increase volume, attract builders, and deepen liquidity across the Perpo ecosystem.

### Growth

- **Point Module for Perpo One:** A unified points system that connects campaigns, quests, and on-chain activity into a single incentive layer. This gives builders a turnkey engagement framework and drives measurable user retention, reducing the cost and effort of sustaining growth programs across the ecosystem.
- **Multi-Level Referral:** Extends the referral model to multiple tiers, enabling communities and builders to create deeper acquisition funnels. This turns each active user into a distribution channel, producing compounding organic growth instead of one-off marketing spend.
- **Perpo Monthly Competition:** A recurring monthly competition cadence that keeps active traders engaged and attracts new participants on a predictable schedule. Regular events drive consistent volume spikes and give builders a reliable calendar to plan promotions around.
- **Expanded RWA Market:** Broadens Perpo's tradable asset universe with real-world-asset-linked perpetuals. Unique instruments differentiate Perpo from competitors, open up a new trader demographic, and give builders exclusive markets unavailable on other venues.

### Trading capabilities

- **Isolated Margin:** Lets traders margin each position independently, capping downside to a single trade instead of the entire account. This attracts professional and high-frequency traders who require granular risk control — directly increasing per-user volume and overall platform utilization.
- **Permissionless Listing:** Removes manual gatekeeping from the market listing process, enabling anyone to propose and spin up new trading pairs. This accelerates market breadth at near-zero operational cost and positions Perpo as the fastest venue to list emerging tokens.

### Platform & developer

- **System Performance Upgrade:** Reduces latency and increases throughput across the matching engine and settlement layer. Better execution quality under load is the single biggest factor for retaining high-volume integrators and attracting institutional flow.
- **Core SDK Enhancements:** Cleaner APIs, richer primitives, and streamlined integration paths that cut builder onboarding time. Less custom glue code means faster go-to-market for new front-ends, directly growing the number of active builders shipping on Perpo.
- **Mobile SDK:** Purpose-built SDK for iOS and Android covering wallet connection, account management, and order flow. This unlocks the mobile DeFi segment — a largely underserved market — for every builder in the Perpo ecosystem.

### Vault upgrades

- **Permissionless Vault:** Opens vault creation to any strategist without manual approval, enabling a wider range of yield strategies to deploy on Perpo. More vaults drive more TVL and deeper liquidity, creating a self-reinforcing flywheel between vault depositors and the trading layer.
- **Non-USDC Vault:** Supports vault deposits in assets beyond USDC — such as ETH, BTC, or LSTs — removing a key friction point for holders who prefer not to convert. This expands the addressable depositor base and increases total capital flowing into Perpo's liquidity layer.
